Cookies Policy

This page explains how Aardvark Anteater Acoustics uses cookies on our website and how you can manage them.

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for basic website functionality, such as remembering your session. They are always active and do not store any personally identifiable information.

2. Analytics & Tracking Cookies

These cookies are only used if you provide consent via the cookie banner. We use them to track anonymous visitor behavior to help us improve our website's performance and content. Currently, we do not use any other third-party tracking services. If we ever add more, we will ask for your consent first.

3. Managing Your Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser's settings. Be aware that blocking essential cookies may affect the website's functionality.

4. Your Consent

The website displays a cookie banner to gain your consent for non-essential cookies. Only after you accept will any analytics or tracking cookies be activated.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please email us at cookies@aaagamescompany.com.
