The Sound is Your Guide

Welcome to All Hallows, a sprawling, mysterious world where the lines between a Halloween party and the afterlife have blurred. As a lost soul, you must navigate a labyrinth of unique, musically-themed zones to uncover the secrets of your past and find your way. This isn't just a game you see; it's a world you hear. Every beat, every melody, and every ambient drone is a clue. Listen closely.

Explore the Zones

The Boiler Room

An industrial underworld of rusted pipes and flickering pressure gauges. The air crackles with the raw energy of high-tempo Drum & Bass. Navigate the machinery and complete rhythmic challenges to appease the spirits of industry.

The Velvet Crypt

A forgotten speakeasy hidden deep within a crypt. Shadows dance to the smooth, melancholic sounds of Cool Jazz. Talk to the lingering specters, solve conversational puzzles, and piece together forgotten memories.

The Mausoleum

A grand, echoing hall of marble and stained glass, transformed into a sanctuary of hypnotic Deep House. The steady 4/4 beat guides you through light-based puzzles and meditative quests of reflection.

We believe in the power of sound to tell stories, create atmosphere, and build worlds.
