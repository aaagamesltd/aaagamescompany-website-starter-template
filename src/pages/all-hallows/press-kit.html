Aardvark Anteater Acoustics Announces 'All Hallows' Coming Exclusively to the Epic Games Store

GLASGOW, SCOTLAND – Aardvark Anteater Acoustics, a new independent game studio, today announced that its highly anticipated debut title, All Hallows, will be released exclusively on the Epic Games Store. The game will be available to add to your Wishlist starting March 17, 2025, with an official launch date set for May 19, 2025.

All Hallows invites players into a mysterious, music-driven world where sound is the key to survival and discovery. Navigate through a unique, sprawling festival of musically-themed zones, solving puzzles and uncovering secrets as a lost soul trying to find its way.

The game will be priced at €39.99.

About Aardvark Anteater Acoustics

Based in Scotland, Aardvark Anteater Acoustics is a new independent game studio with a name as unique as its mission. Founded by a sound designer with a Master's degree in the field, our focus is on crafting unforgettable, high-quality games where audio is not an afterthought, but the very foundation of the experience. We believe in the power of sound to tell stories, create atmosphere, and build worlds.
