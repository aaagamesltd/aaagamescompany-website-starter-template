Dev Blog: Scary Didgeridoo

February 28, 2026

Welcome to the behind-the-scenes of All Hallows’ sound design. In this post, we delve into the creation of the eerie didgeridoo sounds that elevate the game’s atmosphere.

“Sound isn’t just an accessory—it’s a narrative tool. The didgeridoo guides players, evokes tension, and becomes an extension of the horror itself.”

The development process involved recording real instruments, then layering digital effects to create a haunting yet immersive audio experience. The didgeridoo's deep resonance complements our musical zones and heightens player immersion.

Key Techniques

  • Layering live recordings with digital synths
  • Applying reverb and echo for spatial depth
  • Dynamic pitch modulation to evoke suspense

By experimenting with these techniques, our team ensures each area of All Hallows feels alive and sonically unique, making the didgeridoo a centerpiece of the horror experience.
