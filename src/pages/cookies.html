Cookies Policy

This page explains how AAA Games uses cookies on our website and how you can manage them.

Essential cookies

Required for basic website functionality (e.g., remembering your session). These are always active.

Analytics / tracking cookies

These cookies are only used if you accept them via the cookie banner:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks anonymous visitor behavior to improve our website. Expires: 2 years.
  • Other third-party tracking: None currently. If added in the future, we will request consent first.

Managing cookies

You can manage or delete cookies via your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Consent

The website shows a cookie banner for non-essential cookies. Only after you accept will analytics/tracking cookies be activated.
