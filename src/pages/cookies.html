Cookies Policy

This page explains how AAA Games uses cookies on our website and how you can manage them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites function properly and improve your experience.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic functionality, such as remembering your session or user preferences.
  • Analytics or tracking cookies: We do not currently use any third-party tracking cookies. If we add them in the future, we will request your consent.

Managing cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Consent

Our website shows a simple cookie consent banner if non-essential cookies are added in the future. By using the site, you consent to essential cookies being used.

More information

For questions about our cookies or privacy practices, contact us at cookies@aaagamescompany.com.
