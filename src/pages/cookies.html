Cookies Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-11

This Cookies Policy explains how Aardvark Anteater Acoustics Games OÜ (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website, including when promoting the video game All Hallows. It is designed to comply with European (GDPR, ePrivacy) and global legal requirements.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They can store information about your preferences, browsing behavior, and help provide functionality such as remembering your consent choices.

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., navigation, cookie consent storage).
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymous data on website usage to help us improve content and user experience. Example: Google Analytics.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences, such as language or consent choices, to improve the user experience.
  • Marketing / Tracking Cookies: Used by third parties to deliver relevant content, measure ad campaigns, or track website traffic across sites.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. The data collected is anonymized and may include:

  • Device type and browser
  • Pages visited and duration
  • Geographic region
  • Referring websites

Google may store cookies on your device to provide these analytics. No personal identifying information (e.g., names or emails) is collected automatically. Analytics scripts are only loaded after you provide explicit consent via the cookie banner.

For more information, see Google's Privacy & Cookies policies.

4. How we manage consent

When you first visit our website, a cookie banner allows you to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You may change your preferences at any time by clearing cookies in your browser or revisiting the cookie consent settings.

5. Legal basis (EU GDPR & ePrivacy)

If you are in the European Union or European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for using non-essential cookies is your explicit consent. Essential cookies do not require consent but are strictly necessary for website operation.

6. Third-party cookies

We may allow third-party services such as social media platforms, video embeds, or analytics providers to place cookies when you interact with them. We are not responsible for their data collection practices. Please check their privacy policies for details.

7. Global compliance

For visitors outside the EU, we respect similar privacy and cookie laws applicable in their country. Non-essential cookies will only be set with your consent where required.

8. How to control cookies

  • You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies.
  • You can withdraw consent for non-essential cookies via the cookie banner or your browser settings.
  • Be aware that disabling cookies may affect website functionality and some features may not work properly.

9. Updates to this Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or business practices. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest revision.
